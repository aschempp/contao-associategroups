=== Titel
Associate Groups


=== Suchbegriffe
associate,member groups,user groups


=== Teaser
Creates an association table for member and user groups


=== Beschreibung
<p><strong>This extension is intended for developers only!</strong></p>
<p>After installing this extensions, the system will maintain two new tables
called tl_member_to_group and tl_user_to_group. These table hold references for
group assignments, allowing you to be able to search for members/users belonging
to a give group (id).</p>
